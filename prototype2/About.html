Vocalist Anthony Hagan, guitarist Chris Littlejohn and drummer Nik Zanter founded Yearless after member changes shook up the lineup of their once beloved Pets With People Names. The band decided they wanted to take a more aggressive approach to pop punk, citing influences such as Knuckle Puck and The Story So Far, and decided that, although not a complete 180,a name change was in order. After a short search, the trio found guitarist Jacob “Moose” Seemuth, and completed their transformation.
